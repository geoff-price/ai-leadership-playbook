# Good Strategy / Bad Strategy — for AI leaders

A practical decision tool built on one durable idea from *Good Strategy/Bad Strategy* by
Richard Rumelt.

## What it does

It interviews you about a real AI decision, then produces an artifact — a strategy-kernel
one-pager, talking points, a working doc, or a dashboard — that turns your slogans into an
actual strategy (or shows you why you don't have one yet).

## Use it

**In Claude (as a Skill):** drop this folder into your skills directory and invoke
`good-strategy-bad-strategy`.

**Anywhere else (copy-paste):** open [`prompt.md`](./prompt.md), copy it into ChatGPT,
Claude, Copilot, or any assistant, and answer the questions.

## Fork it

A regulated enterprise and a startup face different obstacles. Adapt the questions and
emphasis to your context. Make it yours.
